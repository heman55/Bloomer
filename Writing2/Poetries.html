Welcome to Blommer's Poetry Chest

Dip in the ocean of poetries and find joy of reading.

A poem is a careful distillation of reality, like a dream, but with a logical structure that doesn't have to be guesses at. It is a process of binding words like never before.

As Thomas Dylan beautifully defined poetry: "Poetry is what makes me laugh or cry or yawn, what makes my toenails twinkle, what makes me want to do this or that or nothing."
