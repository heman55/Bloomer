I Need Rest

Neha Sah

English Department,
Surya Sen Mahavidyalaya

I need rest...
Rest from the world
Rest from the stress
Stress of everyday's life pressures.

Here she says this,
There he says that,
People are there
But no one is here
To listen problems.
It is not that corona has affected upon me
But loneliness has killed me...
Since four months.
It is not that before there was no problems
But that problems were automatically solved by
Watching the outside areas...
Area of thousands of faces,
Area where you could find reason for being happy,
Area where you loved by strangers for no reasons
Area where you could talk with nature.
But now their is nothing left
Except mobile phone
Where people don't ask how you are
Just show their status through Whatsapp
Not permanently, will be change in few seconds.
All are busy, I too busy
Busy in my life
But my life is over there
Behind the shelf.
So...I need rest
Rest from the world
Rest from the stress
Stress of everyday's life pressures.
