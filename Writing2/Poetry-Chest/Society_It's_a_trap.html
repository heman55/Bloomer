Society: It's a Trap

Biswajit Roy

Humans live in a society
Do they live amicably?
Religion must be a good reason to live happily
They must have morality.

Where relatives, neighbours are not nasty
They do render good advice invariably!
They be proud of your success overwhelmingly
Society will not judge you on your poverty.

Society will give you parable
Just like Jesus Christ
It will take you into its lap
Now think in reverse what says this fable
Don't look down, you're an equilibrist
Society, it's a trap.
